Essa é a primeira build order que aprendi no jogo e a que mais gosto de fazer, principalmente jogando de Franks. Vou manter em inglês pois isso aproxima o jogo da comunidade internacional.

## Match start

<p>Build 2 houses with 2 vills and another with 1 vill. Scout around the base to find: <strong>8 sheeps</strong>, <strong>2 boars</strong>, berries, deers, <strong>3 golds</strong>, <strong>2 stones</strong>; then, go scout the enemy base.</p>

## Dark Age

- 6 to sheep 🐑 (pop 7)
- 3 on wood 🪵 (1º LC – pop 10)
- 2 to lure boar 🐗 + sheep/boar under TC 🐗/🐑 (pop 12)
- 3 to house 🏠 + mill on berries 🫐 (pop 15)
- 1 to lure 2º boar 🐗 (pop 16)
- 1 to berries 🫐 (4 total – pop 17)
- 1 to house 🏠 + 2º LC 🪵 (pop 18)
- 1 to sheep/boar under TC 🐗/🐑 (10 total – pop 19)
- 2 to wood 🪵 (1º LC; 5 total – pop 21)
- Research loom + click Feudal Age (500 food)

<p>On the way to Feudal:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>3 TC</strong> to <strong>wood</strong> 🪵 on 2º LC (5 in one and 4 on another)</li>
  <li><strong>1 TC</strong> to <strong>house</strong> 🏠 at 40% + <strong>barrack ⚔️</strong> at 60% + <strong>wood</strong> 🪵 (2º LC; 5 total on each).</li>
</ul>

<p>TIP: only start making farms after reaching feudal age, otherwise you will run out of wood to make the stable. Send berry vills to farm as they run out.</p>

<h2 id="feudal-age">Feudal Age</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Build <strong>stable 🐎 with 2 wood vills</strong>, make <strong>scouts</strong></li>
  <li>Research <strong>double bit axe</strong> + <strong>horse collar</strong></li>
  <li><strong>1</strong> to on <strong>wood</strong> 🪵 (total of 10; 5 on each LC)</li>
  <li><strong>7</strong> to <strong>farms</strong> 🌾 (chop TC trees and build farm as soon as hit <strong>60 wood</strong>)</li>
  <li><strong>5</strong> to <strong>gold</strong> 🪙 (around 14’ ~ pop 28)</li>
  <li>Research <strong>wheelbarrow</strong> (when 5 on gold – magic number) + build <strong>blacksmith</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Castle Age</strong> (<em>800 food</em> + <em>200 gold</em>)</li>
</ul>

<p>On the way to Castle:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Research <strong>bloodlines</strong> + <strong>cavalry armor</strong> + <strong>gold mining</strong></li>
  <li>Build <strong>2º stable</strong></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="castle-age">Castle Age</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Make knights</li>
  <li>2 more vills to gold (total of 7 to continue making knights)</li>
  <li>Drop <strong>2 TCs</strong> on <strong>gold + wood</strong></li>
  <li>Research <strong>bow saw</strong></li>
</ul>

<p>TIP: each TC cousts <em>275 wood</em> + <em>100 stone</em>; if you spend the inicial stone you will not be able to build the TCs, unless you collect more stone.</p>

<p>After 3 TCs: drop more stables on the front to continue building scouts. Consider droping siege on the front (especially hills) to breake walls + buildings.</p>

<p>When decision to go Imperial Age: build 2 of <strong>castle</strong>, <strong>university</strong>, <strong>monastery</strong> or <strong>siege workshop</strong>; invest on food + gold. Click Imperial Age (<em>1000 food</em> + <em>800 gold</em>)</p>

<p>Collect the relics and drop castles on the hills to take control of the map and the strategic resources (gold and stone).</p>

<h2 id="imperial-age">Imperial Age</h2>

<p>Continue queueing villagers until ~140, leaving 60 pop to building a good and fully upgraded army.</p>

<p>Expand the base around the corners of the maps and take control of the hills.</p>

<p>Build trebuchets to break the enemy castle and other buildings/walls. Make sure to protect your trbuchets with your troops.</p>

<p>Don’t forget to research the economic and military upgrades to build the strongest army.</p>
